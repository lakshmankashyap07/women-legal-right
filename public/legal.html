Women Legal Rights Information

Key Areas of Women's Legal Rights

Family Law

Marriage, divorce, child custody, alimony, and domestic partnerships.

Workplace Rights

Equal pay, maternity leave, workplace harassment, and discrimination protection.

Domestic Violence

Protection orders, restraining orders, and legal recourse against abuse.

Reproductive Rights

Access to healthcare, family planning, and bodily autonomy.

Education Rights

Equal access to education and protection from gender-based discrimination.

Criminal Justice

Rights when accused of crimes and protection from gender bias in the justice system.

Important Notes
